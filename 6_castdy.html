Book cover design & Illustration

Objective:
Develop a captivating book covers that accurately represent the essence of the content
while intriguing potential readers and enticing them to explore further. The cover should serve as
a visual ambassador for the book, effectively communicating its genre, tone, and themes.

Deliverables:
Initial design concepts:Present a range of creative directions that capture the spirit
of the narrative, incorporating imagery, typography, and color schemes that
align with the author's vision and target audience.
Revision rounds:Collaborate closely with the author to refine the chosen concept,
incorporating feedback to ensure the cover effectively communicates the
intended message and resonates with readers.
Finalized artwork:Deliver high-quality, print-ready cover artwork in the required formats,
including front, back, and spine designs, adhering to industry standards and specifications.
Optional:Provide mockups or 3D renderings of the cover for promotional purposes, allowing
the author to visualize how the design will appear in various contexts, such as
online retailers or bookstore displays.
